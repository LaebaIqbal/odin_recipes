Giotto Cake

Home

DESCRIPTION

I recently tried a cake recipe that everyone absolutely loved. So I thought, why not share it with my community? The preparation is incredibly easy: it's a chocolate cake with a moist base and an irresistible cream made from heavy cream, mascarpone, and Giotto pralines. For those who prefer a fresher taste, you can simply add some strawberries—it's a dream!

INGREDIENTS

Chocolate Sponge Cake

  • 3 Eggwhites
  • 3 EggYolks
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 100 g sugar
  • 60 g flour
  • 20 g cocoa powder
  • 25 g starch
  • 4 g baking powder
  • vanilla extract (optional)

Giotto Cream

  • 200 ml whipping cream
  • 500 g mascarpone
  • 27 Giotto pralines
  • 2-4 tbsp powdered sugar

Additionally

  • milk
  • 9 Giotto pralines
  • strawberries (optional)
  • hazelnuts (optional)

STEPS

Preparing the Sponge Cake

  1. Separate the eggs and beat the egg whites with salt and half of the sugar until fluffy.
  2. In a separate Bowl beat the eggyolks with the remaining sugar until they form a creamy mixture.
  3. Gently fold in the fluffy egg whites in the egg yolk mixture in several stages.
  4. Comine dry ingedients, sift them und gently mix them into the batter.
  5. Por the batter into a round baking dish (20 cm diameter) an bake it in a preheated oven at 180 ℃ for 20-25 minutes.
  6. To keep the cake moist, cover it with a damp kitchen towel immediately after baking and let it cool completely.

Preparing the Giotto Cream

  1. Crush the giotto pralines by hand or bleand them in a food processor.
  2. Briefly whip the mascarpone. Add whipping cream, powdered sugar and the blended giotto pralines. Continue whipping until cream is firm.
  3. Let the Giotto Cream chill in the fridge.

Assembling the Cake

  1. Cut the songe cake into 2 even layers.
  2. Moisten the layer of cake with some milk.
  3. Spread the Giotto Cream onto the first layer. If desired, add some strawberries.
  4. Repeat with the next Layer and decorate as you wish.
  5. Now let it chill in the fridge for 3-4 hours or overnight.

Et Voila! Your delicious Giotto Cake is ready!
