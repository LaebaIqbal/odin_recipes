DESCRIPTION

Get ready to make the best naan you've ever tasted. This unbelievably soft and fluffy Pakistani-style naan is incredibly simple to prepare, and once you try it, you'll never need to buy store-bought naan again.

INGREDIENTS

Dough

  • 500 g flour
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 185 g yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 60 ml warm water
  • 180 ml warm milk

Optional

  • garlic
  • butter
  • coriander

STEPS

  1. In a glass, combine water, sugar and dry yeast and let the mixture sit for 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk flor and salt together. Then add the yeast misture, milk, oil and yoghurt and knead uthe dough for 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth.
  3. Cover the dough with a kitchen towel and let it rise at room temperature for about an hour.
  4. Divide the dough into 12 equal-sized pieces and roll them into smooth balls. Let the balls rest for 5 minutes.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, roll each ball into a thin, circle-shaped naan. Cook the naans one at a time in a medium-heat pan until golden brown spots appear on both sides.
  6. (optional) Mince garilc and coriander and add them to the melted butter and brush the mixture generously over the warm naans.

Et Voila! Your delicious Naan is ready!
