DESCRIPTION

This Chicken Chow Mein is incredibly simple to make, and it tastes even better than your favorite Chinese takeout. Once you try this recipe, you'll never need to order in again. The dish is full of flavor and absolutely delicious. Just give it a tryâ€”you won't regret it!

INGREDIENTS

Chicken Marinade

  • 250 g boneless Chicken
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 2 tsp sesame oil

Chow Mein Sauce

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper

Additionally

  • 2 servings noodles (300g)
  • 1 onion
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 2-3 spring onions
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 carrot
  • 2-3 cabbage leaves
  • 2-3 tbsp cooking oil

STEPS

  1. Thinly slice the chicken. Marinate it with the ingredients listed above.
  2. Thinly slice the vegetables listes above.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the ingredients for the sauce.
  4. Boil the noodles according to the package direction and then drain them well.
  5. Over medium heat saute the chicken and set aside.
  6. In the same Pan saute onion and garlic for a few minutes. Add the remainingvegtables (except for the green onions) and stir-fry .
  7. Add noodles and toss in the sauce.
  8. Finally, add the cooked chicken and the green onions. Toss everything together for one more minute to heat through.

Et Voila! Your delicious Chicken Chow Mein is ready!
